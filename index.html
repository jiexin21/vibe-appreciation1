Gratitude Harvest

🌱 Plant the Praise. Watch Us Grow.

Welcome to the Team!

👋 If you're new here—welcome aboard! Gratitude Harvest is one of the ways we celebrate each other and build a connected, values-driven culture. It's more than a program—it's a chance to feel seen, supported, and part of something meaningful from day one.

How It Works

  • 📩 Nominate any colleague using our online form—yes, even as a newcomer, your voice matters.
  • 🍃 Your nomination becomes a “leaf” on our Gratitude Tree in the lobby.
  • 🎉 Every 4 months, we celebrate “Harvest Heroes” who receive meaningful, thoughtful tokens.

Why It Matters

💡 Starting a new role can feel overwhelming—this initiative ensures you’ll see (and be seen) for the everyday ways people help each other. It’s a great way to get to know your teammates, show appreciation, and feel part of the bigger picture.
